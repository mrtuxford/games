Anagram

N G A A R M A

_ _ _ _ _ _ _

An anagram is a game in which players form words from a group of randomly picked letters. You will see random letters and your task is to unscramble them into the longest word. You can only use each letter once. There is a word that uses all the letters - can you find it?

  1. Set the desired time limit and word category
  2. Hit the green play button to start
  3. Players then call out OR write down the solution
  4. Award points to the person who gets it first OR to everyone who got it after the time limit

Variations include...

  • Splitting the class into teams and the first team to call out the correct anagram gets a point.
  • Write multiple anagrams on the board for individuals or teams to solve. The individual or team with the most correct anagrams after a set time wins.
