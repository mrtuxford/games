Large Numbers

Closest Number puts your mental arithmetic to the test! Can you use the basic mathematical operations (plus/minus and times/divide) to create the target number from the numbers given?

  1. Set the desired time limit and number of big numbers.
  2. Hit the green play button to start the timer and show the target number (e.g. 300).
  3. Use the numbers and basic mathematical operators to get as close as possible to the target number (e.g. 4 - 1 = 3, then 3 * 100 = 300). You do not need to use all of the numbers. You can break up your calculation into steps or use brackets.
  4. When the time is up, the students must stop writing and trade their calculation with another student to verify their answer.
  5. The person with the closest number to the target number wins!

Variations include...

  • Using other mathematical operations such as factorial and exponential.
  • Split the group into teams instead of having an individual competition.
