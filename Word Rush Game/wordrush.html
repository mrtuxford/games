Word Rush

W O R D R U S H

Word Rush is a word game played in pairs or small groups where the goal is to come up with as many words as possible using a given set of letters within a certain time limit. The group with the most words at the end of the time limit is the winner.

  1. Set the desired time limit and word category
  2. Hit the green play button to start the timer and show the key word (e.g. E X A M P L E)
  3. The students must work together to come up with as many words as possible using the given letters (e.g. ample, exam, map, plea, ...). They can use each letter the number of times it appears in the key word and can rearrange the letters to form different words.
  4. When the time is up, the students must stop writing and trade their list of words with another group.
  5. Show the possible solutions on screen and get students to check their work. Note: not all solutions are shown on screen - a dictionary may be required.
  6. The group with most correct words wins!

Variations include...

  • Using letters from the key words multiple times. This drastically increases the number of words and these solutions aren't automatically generated.
  • Restrict the word length to a certain number so only words of that word length are accepted.
