Summary

5min+

Greedy Pig is a dice game that tests how far people are willing to go for points. Can you find the balance between playing it safe to secure your points and taking on the risk to secure victory?

Instructions

  1. Gather two dice OR use digital version.
  2. Determine the number of rounds for the game (5 rounds for a ~5min game).
  3. Everyone stands up to indicate they are in the round.
  4. Roll the dice. The players can choose to stay standing to try to add to their score, but if a double is rolled, they lose all of their points for that round.
  5. Continue rolling the dice until a double is rolled and the round is over.
  6. When a player decides to sit during a round, the score they have accumulated in that round is added to their overall total. The player keeps track of their own score.
  7. Play continues until the set number of rounds have been played.
  8. The player with the highest overall score wins the game.

Variations include...

  • Add more dice or increase the number of rounds.
  • Players roll their own dice instead of as a class.
