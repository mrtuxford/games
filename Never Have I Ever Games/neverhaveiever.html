Never have I ever...

  1. Have everyone sit or stand in a circle.
  2. One person starts by saying a statement beginning with "Never have I ever..." and finishing with something they have never done. For example, "Never have I ever ridden a horse." OR Use the ones generated above.
  3. Everyone who has done the thing mentioned must raise their hand.
  4. The person who made the statement then gets to choose someone who raised their hand to share a story about the time they did the thing mentioned in the statement.
  5. Play continues with the next person making a statement.
  6. It's important to remember to keep the statements appropriate, and to be respectful of other people's experiences and stories. Have fun!

Variations include...

  • Players keep track of their score by starting with 5points (or 10points) shown by the number of fingers displayed. They put a finger down each time they have done the statement. First person with no points left wins (or loses)!
